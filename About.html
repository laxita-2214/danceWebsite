ABOUT US

contact Natanritya | Fatorda | Goa

Siddhant Gadekar is the founder of the Siddhant Gadekar dance& fitness academy. He himself is a very passionate n professional dancer. He started dancing at the age of 5 years, as years passed he grew more as a professional dancer. At school and college he took part in various competitions for school level ,interschool level, college level and all intercollegiate levels and won numerous prizes. Siddhant Gadekar (Sitaram Gadekar) also represented Goa at national level many time(Pune, Ratnagiri ,Mumbai etc.) and won many prizes, after years of devotion, practise and hard work. He thought of taking dance to the next level professional level by opening an academy Siddhant Gadekar dance & fitness academy on 2nd January 2014. So that he can give a new and firm platform for the new young talent in Goa who have potential but no means coming up. From the academy had started he has taken it to such a high level that the academy is recognised with its name from the kids, teens, adults and oldies. Siddhant Gadekar himself not only dances, teaches dance but he worships dance.
